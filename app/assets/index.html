Repcol

Repcol is an experimental service for getting an overview of the strategies at work in collections of art. Which artists were acquired when? How many works were collected? What was their gender?

It currently lets you see the collection of The Norwegian National Museum. The museum has kindly provided us with a complete, work in progress, snapshot of the archive â€“ it does therefore not adhere to their normal quality standards and may contain errors.

Repcol has been made possible with the generous support of The Arts Council Norway and The Norwegian National Museum. Read more about the project on the sites of Bengler or the National Museum.

Navigation

3D Navigation is a bit of a pickle
  • Watch the video on the right for an introduction
  • Click and drag to spin the model
  • Mousewheel or touchpad pinch to zoom
  • Shift key and drag to pan
Selecting artists
  • Double click an artist to focus
  • Double click anywhere to unselect
  • Left and right cursor keys to select previous and next artist
  • Just start typing to search. Esc to stop.
