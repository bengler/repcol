Repcol

Repcol lets you see how representative a collection of art is. Which artists were acquired when? What was their gender? How many works were collected from each artist? Did it happen after their death?

It currently lets you explore the collection of The Norwegian National Museum. The museum has kindly provided us with a snapshot of the entire archive – it does therefore not adhere to their normal quality standards and may contain errors.

Repcol has been made possible with the generous support of The Arts Council Norway and The Norwegian National Museum. Read more about the project on the sites of Bengler or the National Museum.

3D Navigation is a bit of a pickle
  • Watch the video for an introduction
  • Click and drag to spin the model
  • Mousewheel or touchpad pinch to zoom
  • Shift key and drag to pan
Selecting artists
  • Click an artist to focus
  • Click anywhere to unselect
  • Left and right cursor keys to select previous and next artist
  • Just start typing to search. Esc to stop.
